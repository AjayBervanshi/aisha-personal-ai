How Does She Make a Video?

From zero to a live YouTube video — here's every step, in plain English.

1

She picks a topic

Aisha checks what's trending on YouTube, combines it with the channel's theme, and picks a topic that will get views. You can also just tell her: "Make a video about a couple cooking disaster."

2

She writes the script

Uses Google's Gemini AI (one of the world's best AI models) to write a full story script in Hindi or English. If Gemini is busy, she automatically switches to NVIDIA's AI. She always has a backup.

3

She records the voice

Uses ElevenLabs (a professional voice AI) to record the script in Aisha's voice or Riya's voice. These sound like real human voices — warm, emotional, expressive. Not robotic at all.

4

She creates the video

Combines the voice recording with AI-generated images and transitions into an MP4 video file. Like editing in iMovie or Premiere — but zero human involvement.

5

She uploads to YouTube

Logs into YouTube with Ajay's account credentials (securely stored), picks the best title and tags for the algorithm, and publishes the video. Done.

6

She texts Ajay

Sends a Telegram message: "New video live! Here's the link 🎬" — so Ajay knows it's done without having to check anything.

This entire process — from blank page to live YouTube video — happens automatically, every 4 hours, on all 4 channels, while Ajay is sleeping, working, or doing anything else. — Tested and confirmed working today, March 21, 2026
