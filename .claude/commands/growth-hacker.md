# /growth-hacker — Channel Growth Strategy

Produce growth strategy suggestions for YouTube/Instagram channel metrics.

## Usage
`/growth-hacker <channel_or_platform>`

## Steps
1. Analyze current channel state (if YouTube API available)
2. Research trending topics in the niche
3. Output:
   - **Top 5 video ideas** with estimated CTR potential
   - **Upload frequency recommendation**
   - **Thumbnail A/B test suggestions**
   - **SEO keyword opportunities** (low competition, high volume)
   - **Community engagement tactics**
   - **Cross-platform repurposing** (YouTube → Instagram Reels → TikTok)
4. Prioritize by revenue potential (RPM × views)
