Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Story With Aisha ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Our application may collect the following information:

  • Facebook account name and user ID (for authentication)
  • Facebook Page access tokens (to manage and publish content to connected pages)
  • Instagram Business Account ID (to publish content to your Instagram account)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Authenticate and authorize access to your Facebook Page and connected Instagram account
  • Publish content (posts, reels, stories) to your Instagram Business Account on your behalf
  • Manage and schedule content for your social media channels

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored locally on your device and in your personal database. We do not store your tokens on external servers beyond what is necessary for the app to function.

4. Third-Party Services

This application uses the Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Graph API. Your use of Facebook and Instagram features is also governed by:

  • Meta Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy

5. Data Deletion

You can revoke this app's access to your Facebook and Instagram accounts at any time by visiting:

  • Facebook: Settings & Privacy → Settings → Security and Login → Apps and Websites
  • Instagram: Settings → Security → Apps and Websites
